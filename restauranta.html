Pho 501



Pho 501 is a hole-in the wall outfit that serves the community around Pratt & Whitney in East Hartford. They do one thing really well and that is make affordable, delicious pho.


It's Only Natural



It's Only Natural is well known in Hartford County. It's pricey and you need to like a lot of green leafy vegetables. But if you're worried you don't get enough follic acid, this is truly your place. Their menu needs more regular updating though.


Flora



Flora is what ION dreams of being but can't because it's too expensive in Middletown; that is really have a diverse menu for a discerning clientel. While ION fills you with fiber, Flora merges fiber rich veges with healthy fats for a truly satiating experience, along multiple dimensions. Next
